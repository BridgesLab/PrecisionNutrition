# PRISMA-S Checklist 
adapted for this review)

1. **Information sources:** MEDLINE (via PubMed), Embase, Web of Science Core Collection, Cochrane CENTRAL, CINAHL, ClinicalTrials.gov, WHO ICTRP
2. **Dates of coverage:** From inception to September 30, 2025
3. **Search strategies:** Full strategies for each database are documented in separate .txt files
4. **Limits/Restrictions:** Humans only. No date or language limits applied. Excluded publication types: animal-only studies, case reports/series, editorials, narrative reviews
5. **Deduplication:** All records imported to a reference manager and de-duplicated prior to screening
6. **Updating:** Searches will be rerun prior to final analyses to capture newly published studies
7. **Other sources**: Reference list screening and citation chasing of included articles
